## Your Inbox

The PACT inbox shows requests addressed to **you** or to groups you've **subscribed** to.

Requests are grouped by status:

- **Pending** — waiting for your response
- **Active** — in progress
- **Completed** — done
- **Cancelled** — withdrawn by sender

### Background polling

The extension polls for new requests every 5 minutes (configurable via `pact.pollInterval`). You'll see a notification when new requests arrive.

### Quick actions

- Click a request to view its full details
- Use the reply button to respond directly
- Use "Open in AI" to get help from Claude
